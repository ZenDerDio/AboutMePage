My journey with Web Development

Everyday i want to learn something new, something meaningful
New technologies are the main focus of my passion for web development and design

My story with programming started in Junior School, at that time I was in love with RTS games (and I still am!) such as Medieval Total War and Warcraft III, I discovered mods and wanted to create something of my own in my beloved games I started with a simple scripts for maps then moved to balance and full story mods with advanced events and AI modification. I noticed I really enjoy it so I decided to learn programming language. I went for C++ because it seemed popular and had everything I needed. During that time I learned core concepts of programming.

As time passed i got into Game Development with Unity/C# and Unreal Engine/C++, after finishing few projects I wanted to try something different. That was the moment i started my journey with JavaScript, CSS and HTML, i made some websites, then made a simple game using javascript. Another change came soon after that, i moved into Android Development with Java, made project that granted me highest possible grade at university. After first year at university i decided to become Software Developer, despite the fact that most companies ignored, In the end, I could chose where I want to work. I choose a company that allowed me to work as FullStack in Java and Angular/React.

Since i started working my skill level increased at a rate i thought was impossible. During my internship, in internal Project, I was thrown against Angular which i knew nothing about, but i quickly manage to learn that and became quite fluent at it, that was what convinced the company to hire me after internship ended. From that moment i was pursuing mastery in frontend technologies while working on Angular/React projects with clients. I constantly work on my own projects and educate myself so that my code may be cleaner, better and faster.
